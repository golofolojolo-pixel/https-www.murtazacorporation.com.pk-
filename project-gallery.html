Project Gallery

A record of supplied materials, fabrication work, industrial installations and completed client deliveries — with the exact scope, timeline and role Murtaza Corporation played in each.

Hub Power Station, Pakistan

Ishikawajima Harima Heavy Industries 1992

Hub Power Project — Pakistan

Power Generation

The Hub Power Station was the first and largest power station in Southern Asia to be financed by the private sector, and remains one of the largest private power projects developed within the newly industrialised world. Our involvement in this project marked our entry into the major league of hardware suppliers and significantly broadened our expertise in industrial piping.

  • Year Awarded1992
  • SectorPrivate Sector Power Generation
  • DistinctionFirst & largest privately financed power station in South Asia
  • ContractorIshikawajima Harima Heavy Industries

Our role: Appointed as one of the major local pipe and fittings suppliers to the Hub Power Station project — the engagement that established Murtaza Corporation within the major hardware supply sector.

Pak-American Fertilisers Ltd, Mianwali

Toyo Engineering 1999

Extension of Pak-American Fertilisers Ltd — Pakistan

Fertiliser Manufacturing

Pak-American Fertilisers Limited (PAFL), Pakistan's first fertiliser manufacturing concern established in 1959, operates a plant at Mianwali producing 1,050 metric tons of urea per day as a subsidiary of the National Fertiliser Corporation of Pakistan (NFC). The plant extension, commissioned in 1999, was designed by Toyo Engineering Japan to the latest industry standards.

  • Production Capacity1,050 metric tons urea / day
  • Year Commissioned1999
  • ClientPak-American Fertilisers Ltd (subsidiary of NFC)
  • Designed ByToyo Engineering, Japan

Our role: Engaged as one of the major hardware contractors on the plant extension, a relationship with Pak-American Fertilisers Limited that continues to this day.

PARCO Oil Refinery & Port Qasim Power Project

Marubeni Corporation 2000

PARCO Oil Refinery & Port Qasim Power Project

Oil Refining & Power Generation

PARCO is a fully integrated energy company and one of the leading players in Pakistan's corporate sector, with a history spanning over 30 years. Structured as a joint venture between the Governments of Pakistan and Abu Dhabi, the company is regarded as a role model for Pak-Arab business cooperation.

  • Year2000
  • ClientPak-Arab Refinery Limited (PARCO)
  • StructureJoint venture — Governments of Pakistan & Abu Dhabi
  • SectorOil Refining & Power

Our role: One of the few major pipe and fittings contractors engaged on the refinery and power project, with a mutually progressive relationship anticipated for PARCO's future developments.
