Products

Products that meet your requirement.

We don't ask you to fit your project around our stock. Tubes, pipes, fittings, flanges, valves, and structural items — sourced from certified global manufacturers and customized to your exact specification, whether that's one fitting or one hundred tons.

Manufactured and tested to ASTM A312 / A106, ASME B16.9, API 5L, and DIN 2448 standards — with Mill Test Reports available on request for full material traceability.

  • Available in Seamless & Welded
  • Sizes: 1/4" – 24"
  • Material: Stainless & Carbon Steel
  • Grades: 304, 304L, 316, 316L, A106 Gr. B
  • Thickness: SCH10, SCH40, SCH80 & SCH160

Pipes

Certified sizes and grades, imported and local stock.
  • Available in Butt Weld, BSP & NPT
  • Sizes: 1/4" – 24"
  • Material: Stainless & Carbon Steel
  • Grades: 304, 316, A105 & A106
  • Pressure: SCH10–SCH80 & 150LB–6000LB

Fittings

Elbows, tees, reducers, and couplings, ready to spec.
  • Available in Ball, Gate, Globe, Check & Butterfly
  • Sizes: 1/2" – 20"
  • Material: Stainless, Carbon & Mild Steel
  • Grades: 304, 316, CF8, CF8M & A105
  • Pressure: 150LB–2500LB, PN10–PN40 & 10K

Valves

Pressure-rated valves for industrial flow control.
  • Available in Blind, SORF, WNRF & MS Flanges
  • Sizes: 1/2" – 32"
  • Material: Stainless & Mild Steel
  • Grades: 304, 304L, 316, 316L & A105
  • Pressure: Class 150–1500, PN10, PN16 & 10K

Flanges

SS/MS flanges in standard and custom pressure ratings.
  • Available in Round, Square, Rectangle & Boiler Tubes
  • Sizes: 6mm – 4"
  • Material: Stainless & Mild Steel
  • Grades: 304, 304L & 316L
  • Thickness: 1.5mm, 2mm & 3mm

Tubes

Instrumentation and general-purpose tubing, certified stock.
  • Available in Tubes, Fittings & Valves
  • Sizes: 3/4" – 4" (SMS Standard)
  • Material: Stainless Steel 316L
  • Finish: Dairy Polished
