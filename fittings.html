Stainless & Carbon Steel Fitting Systems

Pipe fittings allow alterations in flow direction, size reduction, closure or branching within a piping system, and are produced either by welding pipe sections together (butt-weld) or by machining threaded and socket-weld connections for mechanical joining. Stainless steel fittings offer excellent corrosion resistance and are well suited to harsh, corrosive or hygienic environments, while carbon steel fittings — which can be coated or treated to improve corrosion resistance — remain a cost-effective, weld-friendly choice where corrosion resistance is not the primary concern. Both materials perform reliably across a wide temperature range and see use across petrochemical, chemical processing, oil and gas, water treatment and general industrial applications.

Fittings for fluid applications in petrochemical, desalination, mineral processing, power generation and oil & gas recovery plants, as well as industrial, structural and decorative applications, are stocked across a comprehensive range of sizes, wall thicknesses, grades and connection types to suit pipeline pressure and end-use requirements. The Murtaza Corporation stock range has been built over many years of experience to meet these demands.

Butt-weld Fittings

Formed by welding directly to pipe ends, butt-weld fittings — elbows, tees, end caps, reducers, crosses, collars and stub ends — enable changes in flow direction, size reduction, closure or branching across diverse industries. Nominal pipe sizes and wall thicknesses are matched to the pipeline's pressure requirements, and fittings are supplied in stainless steel, carbon steel, alloy steel and nickel alloys, in both seamless and welded (including EFW) construction, sizes 1/4" NB to 32" NB, wall thickness SCH-5S to SCH-XXS.

Threaded Fittings — NPT, BSP & Socket-weld

Threaded and socket-weld fittings are vital in piping systems where mechanical, dismantlable connections are required. NPT fittings, with tapered threads, excel in high-pressure scenarios and are widely used in the United States; BSP fittings, with parallel threaded ends, suit lower-pressure applications and are common across oil & gas and general industry. Available in elbows, tees, unions, crosses, couplings, caps, bushings, plugs, nipples and specialty outlet fittings (weld-o-let, elbow-o-let, sock-o-let, thread-o-let and more), in sizes 1/8" NB to 4" NB, at pressure ratings from 150 lb up to 9000 lbs.

Spec: ASTM A105, A350 LF2 (CS) · ASTM A182 F304/F304L/F316/F316L/F347L/F321 (SS) · ASTM A182 F11 (Alloy) · Manufactured to ANSI B16.11 / BS 2.1, galvanised or pickled finish
